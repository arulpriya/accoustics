Research & Experience

Research Focus Areas

01

Structural Vibrations & FEA

Advanced numerical simulations focusing on modal analysis and frequency response for aerospace and civil structures using industry-standard finite element methods.

02

Analytical Wave Propagation

Deep research into classical wave equations and their application in complex media, optimizing noise reduction through rigorous analytical calculations.

03

CAD & Acoustic Coupling

Interdisciplinary models bridging geometric CAD accuracy with behavioral acoustic performance to predict energy transfer in fluid-structure interfaces.

04

Nonlinear Structural Dynamics

Exploring non-linear effects in structural vibrations, including large-scale deformation and contact dynamics in specialized engineering environments.
